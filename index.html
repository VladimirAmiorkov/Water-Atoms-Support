Water Atoms

Staying hydrated made easy

"Do I drink enough water?" "Am I hydrated?" "I forgot to drink water again!", this are just a few questions that we may ask ourselves very often. We all know drinking water is very important and we tell ourselves that today I will drink enough water. This is easer said than done. As we get swooped by our daily tasks and lives and we forget this simple but very important task, to drink enough water. And this is the core idea behind "Water Atoms", a simple, easy and fast to use app with a simple purpose "Make sure that your are correctly hydrated during your day."

Features

  • Integrated with Apple Health App
  • Add/remove water intake entries (stored in your Apple Health account)
  • Get personalized reminders to drink water during your day (can be disabled entirely or only for the current day)
  • Customize your wake up and go to bed times
  • Customize the default "watner intake" size
  • Add/change your weight (stored in your Apple Health account)
  • Apple Health activities automatically adjust your daily water intake
